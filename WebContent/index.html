Wellnet Australia Pty Ltd has developed the Wellnet Wine Trade Management System. The system assists in the procurement, promotion and distribution of Australian wine to international markets.

Wellnet is a wine distribution company that develops international markets for more than 20 award-winning wineries from Australia's premium wine regions. Around 95 per cent are West Australian, with the focus on small, boutique, family-run wineries.
